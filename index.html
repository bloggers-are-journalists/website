Bloggers are journalists.

We want to create an ondemand newspapers service that delivers blogger content to readers in printed format in a weekly periodical. The newpapers will be customized to the user and the users will be able to vote and rate the articles online to help guide the printing. This service will turn bloggers into journalists and help protect them under the law. The readers will not only get great readable collecter items, but also be supporting bloggers and protecting them from legal problems.
